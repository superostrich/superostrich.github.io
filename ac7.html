Overview

These texture mods replace one or more of the default aircraft skins in Ace Combat 7.

To install, simply place the downloaded .pak file into ..\steamapps\common\ACE COMBAT 7\Game\Content\Paks\~mods

F-104C -Bare Metal-

This pack replaces the default Osean & Trigger skins with bare-metal liveries inspired by the Starfighters of the 1960's.

F-2A -502-

This pack features liveries based on JASDF F-2A No. 63-8502 from the Air Development and Test Wing, as well as Flyuger from Ace Combat 6. These replace Color 3.

There are three .pak files included;

  • f2a-502-noemblems.pak - 502 Livery with no emblems
  • f2a-502-adtw.pak - F-2A No. 502 with JASDF Air Development Test and Wing emblems
  • f2a-flyuger.pak - Flyuger from Ace Combat 6, with Estovakian Air Force markings
