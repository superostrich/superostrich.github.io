Ace Combat Joint Assault - HQ Emblem Kit

Last Updated on November 6, 2018

Download Emblem Kit v1

Overview

This kit replaces all of the 32x32px emblems found in the hangar and missions, with the 64x64px emblems found in the data-viewer. It also allows you to easily replace any of the stock emblems with your own by simply overwiring any of the files found in the \emblems\ folder.

Installation

  1. Extract the contents of the zip file to ppsspp\memstick\PSP\TEXTURES\ULUS10511
  2. In PPSSPP, check 'Replace Textures' under Settings→Tools→Developer_Tools


  3. Turn off 'Texture Scaling' under Setting→Graphics→Texture_Scaling
